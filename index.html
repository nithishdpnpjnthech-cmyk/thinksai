How does Thinkz AI work?

Thinkz AI ingests data from your CRM, email, and communication tools, then applies models trained on millions of B2B sales motions to surface signals, prioritize accounts, and automate routine work.

Can Thinkz AI integrate with existing systems?

Yes. We offer native, bi-directional integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Salesloft, Gong, Slack, and most major data warehouses.

Is Thinkz AI suitable for startups?

Absolutely. We have a Starter tier built for teams under 25 reps and scale all the way to enterprise deployments.

How quickly can businesses get started?

Most teams go live within 7 days. Enterprise rollouts with custom workflows typically complete in 3–4 weeks.

Does Thinkz AI provide analytics and reporting?

Yes — a full analytics suite with dashboards, cohort breakdowns, forecast variance tracking, and exportable reports for executives.
